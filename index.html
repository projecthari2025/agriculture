Alphonso Mango Tree

The Alphonso mango tree is renowned for producing one of the most delicious and sought-after mango varieties in the world. These majestic trees can grow up to 30-40 feet tall and have a dense, rounded canopy that provides ample shade.

Key Features:

  • Height: 30-40 feet when fully grown
  • Fruit Season: March to July
  • Fruit Color: Golden yellow when ripe
  • Nutritional Value: Rich in Vitamins A, C, and antioxidants
